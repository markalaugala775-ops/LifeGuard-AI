🩹 Emergency First Aid Guide

Follow these basic first-aid steps while waiting for medical help.

❤️ Heart Attack

• Call 108 immediately.

• Keep the person calm.

• Loosen tight clothing.

🩸 Severe Bleeding

• Apply firm pressure.

• Use a clean cloth or bandage.

• Raise the injured area if possible.

🔥 Burns

• Cool with running water for 20 minutes.

• Do not use ice.

• Cover with a clean dressing.

🦴 Fracture

• Keep the injured area still.

• Do not move the bone.

• Wait for medical assistance.

⚡ Electric Shock

• Turn off the power source.

• Do not touch the victim directly.

• Call emergency services immediately.

🚑 Emergency Number

108

Call immediately in case of emergency.
